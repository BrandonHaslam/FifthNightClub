Frequently Asked Questions

When are the photos going to be posted and where? All photos from each night will be posted as soon as possible, this is dependent on when our photographer gets the opportunity to upload them. All photos will be uploaded to relevant albums on the Fifth Nightclub Facebook page. Do you do any deals for birthdays? We have just introduced tables and will soon be offering packages for any birthday or group bookings, for birthdays if you contact us either via the group booking page or Facebook letting us know how many people to expect and when, we can always accommodate something for you! Do you have a cloak room? Yes we do! We charge £2 per item, each item has to be put in separately, we do not allow multiple items on the same hanger. I lost/left something in the club, how do I check if it’s there and get it back? For lost property, please email Hello@fifthmanchester.com we will reply as soon as possible. If you have left an item in the cloak room and still have your wristband, please include this as it makes it easier! I want to organise a social for a society, how do I do this? For socials, please message us either via the group booking page or Facebook, let us know the number of people you are expecting to bring and when, we will get back to you as soon as we can!
